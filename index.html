JODHPUR WALK With KUKU

Welcome, mighty explorers & travelers! It will be my pleasure to guide you through the incredible Jodhpur.

A favorite destination of international celebs for its precious royalty and heritage. The breathtaking architecture, history, food, people, handicrafts, royal hotels, palaces, and ponds - the reasons are endless. While I can't spill all the beans, please get in touch with us.

Discover walks

Jodhpur sight seeing

-visit Jaswant Thada, Mehrangarh Fort (optional zip line tour). Visit Umaid Bhawan Palace.

Blue city walk

-First we will visit Pachetiya Hill for sunrise Then, walk towards Fateh Pol, Padamsar/ Ranisar, Chand Baodi, Jetta Bera Well, Blue House Lanes, Nav Chokiya, Juni Mandi, Pongal Pada, Sarafa Market, Sonaro ki Ghati, Kunj Bihari ji Temple, Lakhara Market, Toorji ka Jhalar, Gulab Sagar and Clock Tower.

Osian camel safari

Osian (also spelt Osiyan) is an amazing desert safari place and historical town located off the Jodhpur-Jaisalmer train route. It is an oasis in the Thar known for 12th century Hindu & Jain temple. If you are looking for camel ride and luxury desert camping near Jodhpur? Osian is perfect place to go Glamping!.

Bishnoi village safari tour

Learn about the village’s history and culture from an informative local guide while visiting with weavers, potters, and other local craftsmen and women. Enjoy a home-cooked meal with a local family and complimentary water, tea, and coffee.

Cycling tour

Experience this exciting and eco-friendly tour around the streets of Jodhpur. Go out for a 1 hrs cycling adventure around the alleys and the fort wall and get to know the different communities and its history. Here you will be introduced to the culture and life of today by our local site guide. Cycle through the alleys of the old blue city and experience it coming to life as localities start their.

Jawai Leopard Safari

Visit for crocodiles, Birding and exploring the western Rajasthan’s largest water reservoir after Jawai Leopard Reserve, where the chances of spotting leopards are almost confirmed, while searching leopards in Jawai hills you will experience thrilling hill drives and a beautiful moment of sunset in Jawai.
