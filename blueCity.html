Blue City Walk

Discover the history and heritage of Jodhpur on a 2-hour walking tour led by an informative guide. Stroll through the lanes of Jodhpur, aka the Blue City, where forts, palaces, temples, and havelis. Find Instagram-worthy spots, well, imaginative murals, and intricately carved windows have been painted in vivid shades of blue. Shop traditional Rajasthani arts, crafts, apparel, footwear, or accessories Meet some local people along the way and hear insightful commentary about the sights from your guide.
